## FAQ

**Q: Is this actually free?**
A: Yes. No login, no credit card, no catch. StackAudit is a
free tool built by Credex — we make money when we help you
buy AI credits at a discount, not by charging you for the audit.

**Q: How accurate is the pricing data?**
A: Every price in the audit engine is sourced from the official
vendor pricing page and verified weekly. Sources are listed in
our open PRICING_DATA.md on GitHub. If something is wrong,
you can file an issue.

**Q: Do you store my data?**
A: Your email (if you choose to share it) and audit results
are stored securely in our database. Your audit has a unique
shareable URL but company name and email are stripped from
the public version. We don't sell your data.

**Q: What if my tool isn't on the list?**
A: We currently support Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Claude,
ChatGPT, Anthropic API, OpenAI API, Gemini, and Windsurf.
More tools are added based on demand. Email us to request one.

**Q: What is Credex and why are they offering this free?**
A: Credex sells discounted AI infrastructure credits sourced
from companies that overforecast. StackAudit is our free
diagnostic tool — if your audit shows significant savings,
we'll show you how Credex credits can save you even more.
If you're already spending optimally, we'll tell you that too.
