## What a Converted Lead is Worth to Credex

Credex sells discounted AI infrastructure credits.
Assume average deal size: $2,000 in credits purchased.
Credex margin on resale: estimated 15–25%. Call it 20%.
Revenue per converted customer: $400.

If a customer buys credits quarterly: $1,600/year LTV.
If they refer one other customer: $3,200 LTV.

Conservative estimate: **$400 revenue per converted lead.**
Optimistic (repeat buyer): **$1,600 LTV per customer.**

## Conversion Funnel

Audit completed → Email captured: **25%**
(Value shown first, email asked second — higher than typical)

Email captured → Credex consultation booked: **8%**
(Only high-savings audits >$500/mo get the Credex CTA)

Credex consultation → Credit purchase: **30%**
(Warm lead, specific savings identified, Credex is the solution)

Full funnel: 100 audits → 25 leads → 2 consultations → 0.6 sales

To get 1 sale: need ~170 audits completed.
At $400/sale: need $0 CAC → profitable from audit #1.

## What Would Have to Be True for $1M ARR in 18 Months

$1M ARR = $83,333/month revenue to Credex.
At $400 revenue per customer and 1 purchase/quarter:
Need ~208 new customers/month.

Funnel math:
- 208 customers/month at 30% close rate = 693 consultations/month
- 693 consultations at 8% conversion = 8,663 leads/month
- 8,663 leads at 25% capture rate = 34,650 audits/month

**34,650 audits/month = ~1,155 audits/day.**

Is this achievable in 18 months?

With viral sharing (each audit has a shareable URL), 
SEO from audit result pages, and Credex's existing
customer base as seed distribution — yes, with one
condition: the tool needs to be featured on at least
2 high-traffic developer newsletters (TLDR, Bytes.dev,
or similar) in the first 90 days.

One newsletter feature = 50,000 impressions = 2,500 audits
at 5% CTR. Two features in 90 days = 5,000 audits baseline.
Viral coefficient of 1.2 (each user shares with 1.2 others)
compounds to ~34,000 audits/month by month 12.

**The math works. The constraint is distribution, not product.**
